Congratulations, friends!

You are Labber, a recent Lighthouse Labs graduate who, after 3 months of extensive study, have finally found some time to close your eyes and get some well-deserved sleep.

But just as you're about to drift off, you find yourself thrown into a fantasy world, full of monsters, traps, and other mysterious dangers.

Missing curly braces, recursion, and undefined variables don't sound too bad now, do they?

Thanks for playing!

Controls

Labber
Movement
  • LEFT / RIGHT - Move
  • UP - Jump
Attacks
  • SPACE - Basic
  • X - Fireball

Available attacks unlock as you progress.

L1 Basic
L2+ Fireball

Enemies

Close combat

These enemies won't go out of their way to attack you, but they will do damage on contact. Watch your step and be careful how close you get!

Plant
Bat
Mushroom

Ranged

These enemies are ranged attackers. In addition to doing damage on contact, they shoot/throw a variety of projectiles.


Snake

The snake is a magical being with the ability to shoot ice beams. Watch out, as it shoots icy projectiles which do significant damage!


Flying Eye

The flying eye patrols from the sky and throws explosive bombs made from the eyes of its fallen siblings.


Goblin

The goblin is a fast enemy. It runs chaotically as it throws bombs, keeping threats at a distance.
