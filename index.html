Controls

Movement
  • LEFT / RIGHT - Move
  • UP - Jump
Attacks

Your current attack changes depending on the level.

  • SPACE - Attack

Enemies

Bat

The bat is a patroling enemy. It won't go out of its way to attack you, but getting hit by a bat will leave you with some damage.

Snake

The snake is a magical being with the ability to shoot ice beams. Watch out, as it shoots icy projectiles which do significant damage!
